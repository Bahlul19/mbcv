Personal Stories

Watch and listen to patients sharing their experiences about living with blood cancer. The variety of ages and backgrounds reflects how many different types of blood cancers there are, and how they can affect anyone, at any time. Each person has their own story to tell, but all with the shared goal to achieve greater awareness and to #MakeBlood CancerVisible.
